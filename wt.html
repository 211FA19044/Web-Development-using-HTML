I am Devi Sri Prasad a music composer in Tollywood. I have worked on over 100 films over the span of 20 years.I have won several awards, including ten Filmfare Awards, nine of which are Best Music Director Telugu and one Special Award for Best Background Music, five CineMAA Awards, seven SIIMA Awards and one Nandi Award. I am one of the highest paid music composers in Tollywood.I received most of his awards and nominations for his compositions in the films Nuvvostanante Nenoddantana, Arya 2, Attarintiki Daredi, S/O Satyamurthy, Srimanthudu, Nannaku Prematho and Rangasthalam2.
